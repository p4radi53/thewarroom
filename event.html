The Event

Warsaw Consulting Conference

... is a reputable Management Consulting Conference in Poland gathering highly-talented CEMS MIM students from leading business schools. During the event, Participants will be faced with challenging case studies as well as networking possibilities. The event offers a possibility to obtain CEMS Skill Seminars.

2020 Online Form of the Conference

In the light of the COVID-19 pandemic, the event will be online, which enables everyone to participate in it from anywhere in the world. Not only will participants take part in real business case studies organized by our corporate partners, but also they will have less formal occasions to interact.
