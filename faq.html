Warsaw Consulting Conference 2020 is one of the biggest Management Consulting Conferences in Poland focused on CEMS MIM students. The WARroom bridges the gap between theory in the classroom & companies reality by confronting students with real business case studies. The event is also an opportunity to meet potential employers and discover new career paths.

In 2020 the WARroom Conference starts on 18th and ends on 19th of November. For the first time the event will be conducted online which gives an opportunity for the people all around the world to participate from the comfort of their own home.

To be eligible for participation, you must be currently enrolled in CEMS Master in International Management.
